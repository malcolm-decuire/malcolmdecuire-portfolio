## Situation:
Snowflake was expanding its Data Clean Room (DCR) product offering, which required technical solutions that aligned with customer business requirements, while showcasing the full potential of DCR through both UI and developer APIs.

## Task:

- Build and demonstrate customized workflows and technical solutions using Snowflake DCR features.
- Lead customers through the sales cycle, from demos to proof of concept, ensuring effective design and implementation of Snowflake DCR solutions.
- Partner with cross-functional teams (Sales, Product, Engineering) to refine and improve Snowflake’s DCR offering.
- Engage technical audiences to build custom applications and leverage machine learning models using Snowflake developer APIs.

## Action:

- Conducted in-depth technical discussions with customers, gathering their requirements and aligning Snowflake DCR features with their business objectives.
- Presented Snowflake DCR capabilities and roadmap to C-level executives and technical teams, positioning the product as a leading solution in data clean rooms.
- Developed tailored workflows and demonstrated API-driven solutions to meet unique customer needs, guiding them from proof of concept to full implementation.
- Collected feedback from customers and prospects and collaborated with internal teams to drive product improvements.
- Created custom analysis templates and machine learning models using Snowflake developer APIs, enabling more efficient data clean room operations for clients.

## Result:

- Helped customers unlock the full potential of Snowflake DCR, resulting in high customer satisfaction and successful product implementations.
- Strengthened Snowflake’s positioning with prospects by effectively communicating the technology’s value and aligning it with business outcomes.
- Contributed to continuous improvement in Snowflake DCR offerings based on customer feedback, ensuring the product remained competitive and innovative.
