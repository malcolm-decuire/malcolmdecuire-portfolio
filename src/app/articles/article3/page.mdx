## Situation:
MiQ needed my team to build a scalable web application to drive healthcare solutions, optimize media planning, and enhance TV Intelligence through technical expertise across multiple teams and products.

## Task:

- Collaborate with Engineering to design and implement a scalable application using Databricks and AWS.
- Serve as the Technical SME and product expert in the pre/post sales process, supporting high-revenue initiatives.
- Develop an ELT workflow within Databricks to improve cross-channel media planning and overall system efficiency.
- Optimize the performance of the TV Intelligence application.
## Action:

- Designed and deployed the application on AWS & React to address specific healthcare business challenges.
- Partnered with cross-functional teams (Sales, Product, Marketing, Engineering) to accelerate feature delivery and reduce time-to-market.
- Developed Databricks workflows and optimized SQL queries for improved system performance and data integration with external systems.
- Created case studies and responded to RFPs, contributing to a $10M line of business in political advertising.
- Delivered technical demonstrations to showcase the business impact of potential solutions.
## Result:

- Generated $5M in partnership commitments through successful healthcare solution deployment.
- Reduced end-to-end product development time by 25%, ensuring faster feature rollouts.
- Secured $10M in revenue by enhancing media planning and exchange processes with the Databricks integration.
- Improved TV Intelligence performance, directly impacting business efficiency.
